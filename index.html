About

Hello, I'm Paria. I am a Graphic and UX Designer.

I have a background in liberal arts, studio art, and music production. I am currently enrolled in a UX/UI Design bootcamp program through Columbia University and am looking for full-time work in the web design field.

More about how I became a graphic designer~

I graduated in 2017 from Georgia State University with a Bachelor of Art in Philosophy. Following graduation, I began to create my digital portfolio by designing concert posters for local shows. This led to my first full-time role as a Jr.

Graphic Designer at a print shop in Brooklyn, NY named SaveMor Digital Printing. Under my brilliant Sr. Designer there, I learned about typography, client management, brand guidelines, and print design. Over the last four years, I have worked full-time and freelance graphic design roles, with six months of my work unique to focusing on UX design.
